Written as: `<prefix>whois <@user>`
Where `<@user>` is a parameter of a discord user.

Valid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>whois Wilson`
:white_small_square: `<prefix>whois @Wilson`

Invalid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>whois`
:white_small_square: `<prefix>whois @UserNotInServer`
