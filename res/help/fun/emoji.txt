Written as: `<prefix>emoji <:emoji:>`
`<:emoji:>` is a server's custom emoji, not a standard unicode one
Command aliases: `<prefix>e`

Valid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>emoji :custom_emoji:`

Invalid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>emoji`
:white_small_square: `<prefix>emoji :blush:` where `:blush:`
