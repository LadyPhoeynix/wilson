Written as: `<prefix>avatar <@user>`
Where `<@user>` is an optional parameter of a discord user.
Leave blank for your own avatar
Command aliases: `<prefix>av`

Valid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>avatar`
:white_small_square: `<prefix>avatar Wilson`
:white_small_square: `<prefix>avatar @Wilson`

Invalid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>avatar @UserNotInServer`
