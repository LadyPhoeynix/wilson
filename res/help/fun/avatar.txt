Written as: `|avatar <@user>`
Where `<@user>` is an optional parameter of a discord user.
Leave blank for your own avatar
Command aliases: `|av`

Valid Examples:
:white_small_square: `|avatar`
:white_small_square: `|avatar Wilson`
:white_small_square: `|avatar @Wilson`

Invalid Examples:
:white_small_square: `|avatar @UserNotInServer`
