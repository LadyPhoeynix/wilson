Written as: `<prefix>dice <number_sides>`
`<number_sides>` is the number of sides on the dice.
Leaving the argument blank will default to a D6 dice.

Valid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>dice`
:white_small_square: `<prefix>dice 10`

Invalid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>dice not_a_number`
