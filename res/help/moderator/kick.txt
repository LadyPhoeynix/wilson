**Permissions Required: Kick Users**
Written as: `|kick <@user> <reason?>`
Kick user `<@user>` with optional reason `<reason?>`

Valid Examples:
:white_small_square: `|kick @user`
:white_small_square: `|kick Wilson`

Invalid Examples:
:white_small_square: `|kick`
:white_small_square: `|kick @UserNotInServer`
:white_small_square: `|kick @user x` where `x` is greater than 7
