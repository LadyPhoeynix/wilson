**Permissons Required: Manage Emojis**
Written as: `<prefix>clone <:custom_emoji:>`
Command aliases: `<prefix>steal <prefix>yoink`

Valid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>clone :custom_emoji:`

Invalid Examples:
:white_small_square: `<prefix>clone`
:white_small_square: `<prefix>clone :emoji_already_in_server:`
