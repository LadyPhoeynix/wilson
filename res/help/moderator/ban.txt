**Permissons Required: Ban Users**
Written as: `|ban <@user> <reason?>`
Ban user `<@user>` with optional reason `<reason?>`

Valid Examples:
:white_small_square: `|ban @user reason`
:white_small_square: `|ban Wilson`

Invalid Examples:
:white_small_square: `|ban`
:white_small_square: `|ban @UserNotInServer`
